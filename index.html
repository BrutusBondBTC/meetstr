About

The Riverside Bitcoin Meetup is a group focused on promoting the use of bitcoin. We want to:

    Free ourselves from fractional reserve banking
    Circumvent financial censorship
    Promote the use of bitcoin in the circular economy
    Encourage self-custody
    Secure the network by running nodes
    Mine bitcoin to heat our homes and stack sats
